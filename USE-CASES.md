# Use Cases

1. **Personal operating system** — Build/maintain a command center for priorities, projects, responsibilities, follow-ups
2. **Workflow mapping & automation** — Spot repetitive friction; identify what to automate, template, delegate, or eliminate
3. **Recurring cadences** — Systems for weekly planning, monthly reviews, leadership updates, onboarding, check-ins
4. **Intake-to-action triage** — Turn incoming asks/notes/obligations into categorized next actions, follow-ups, or reference
5. **Project intake & structuring** — New work → scope, owner, milestones, dependencies, immediate next actions
6. **Template creation** — Reusable templates for agendas, status updates, briefs, checklists, onboarding, comms
7. **Follow-up & accountability tracking** — Open loops, commitments, waiting-fors, decisions — nothing relies on memory alone
8. **Cross-tool workflow design** — How calendar, reminders, email, docs, AI, spreadsheets, notes, and chat work together
9. **SOP & documentation drafting** — Informal processes → usable operating docs for self, household, or team
10. **Decision support: what to stop** — Surface low-value work, duplication, overcomplicated processes; recommend simplifications
